Make this a home-screen app (iPhone):

  1. Open this file in Safari on your iPhone.
  2. Tap the Share icon (square with an up-arrow).
  3. Choose Add to Home Screen.
  4. Tap Add. It now opens full-screen like an app.

To get the file onto your phone: AirDrop it from your Mac, or put it somewhere it opens in Safari. Easiest long-term is to host it free (I can help) so it has a real link.

Heads-up on iOS: sound needs you to tap a button first (already handled — just press Start). Buzzing/haptics don’t work in a Safari web app, so it’s beeps + visuals only.
